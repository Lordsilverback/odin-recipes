Lasagna

Time

Prep Time: 30 mins

Cook Time: 2 hrs 30 mins

Additional Time: 15 mins

Total Time: 3 hrs 15 mins

Servings: 12

Ingredients simplified

  • Meat: sweet or spicy Italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • Onion and Garlic: to taste. Dont be shy
  • Tomato: one can Crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar
  • Spices and seasonings: Fresh or dried parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles: Store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • Egg: Egg.

Instructions Simplified

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the cheese mix.
  4. Layer the lasagna (meat, cheese, pasta, repeat)
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 50 minutes at 375 degrees F.
  6. Let lasagna rest for 15 minutes before serving.
