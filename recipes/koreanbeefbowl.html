Korean-beef-bowl

Time

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 10 mins

Additional Time: NONE

Total Time: 20 mins

Servings: 4

Ingredients simplified

  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger (minced)
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/4 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red-pepper flakes, or more if you like
  • green onions for garnish, if desired
  • sesame seed for garnish, if desired

Instructions Simplified

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet. Add in beef crumbling as you cook it and saute until brown, 4-5 minutes. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Add in garlic and ginger and cook for an additional minute.
  3. Meanwhile in a small bowl whisk together brown sugar, soy sauce, sesame oil and red pepper flakes.
  4. Stir the sauce mixture into the beef and simmer for 2-3 minutes until the sauce is heated through and thickened.
  5. Sprinkle with green onions and sesame seeds for garnish, if desired.
    Serve immediately over a bed of white rice.
